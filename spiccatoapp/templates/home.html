About Spiccato

Spiccato Tunes Music School, founded in 2009, is located in Kitengela, Milimani B Road, Gatanza Building, 1st Floor. With experience of over 15 years, We pride ourselves in offering top-tier music education led by a team of highly skilled, dedicated and passionate instructors.

Mission Statement

To nurture and empower musicians of all ages and backgrounds through high-quality, creative, and personalized music education, fostering a lifelong passion for music and artistic excellence.

Vision

To be a leading music institution recognized for cultivating talent, inspiring performance, and shaping a vibrant musical culture that transforms individuals and communities.

Overall Objective

To provide a comprehensive, inclusive, and performance-driven music education program that equips students with technical skills, artistic expression, and the confidence to succeed in both personal and professional musical journeys.
